Clinic Website Design

UI/UX Design

Client: Healing Hands Walk-in Clinic

About the Project

The Healing Hands Walk-in Clinic website project was designed using Figma, with a strong focus on UX design, accessibility, and brand identity. I created an intuitive interface, ensured ADA compliance for inclusivity, and designed a custom logo to reflect the clinic's trust and care values.

Project Overview

The website was designed to provide patients with a user-friendly platform to access essential clinic information and schedule appointments. This project focused on delivering an intuitive interface, responsive design, and cohesive branding while adhering to the client’s request for simple functionality without a backend system. Appointments were made available through phone and email.

As a web designer, I collaborated with a teammate who developed the site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while I focused on the design, accessibility considerations, and logo creation.

Objectives

  • - Design an intuitive and visually appealing website to enhance patient engagement.
  • - Provide clear and accessible appointment scheduling options (via phone and email).
  • - Create a professional brand identity, including a logo that reflects the clinic's values.
  • - Ensure responsive design for optimal performance on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
